Tutorial

This application allows you to get a "score" for a company and how they might perform based on qualitative data, such as news.

To use it, simply type in the name of any company in the search bar and hit search. Then a list of articles will appear in the center of the page, which you can click on an view the article directly. The score for this company, as well as some basic stock info, will also appear in the top right side of the page. Click on either of them to get more details.
